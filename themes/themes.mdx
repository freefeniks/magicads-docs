## Introduction
MagicAds comes with a clean and modern default theme available for everyone. But you can change your frontend and dashboard themes via obtaining a new one at the **Themes** tab in the admin panel as you prefer.

## Theme Configuration
To be able to change your theme, follow these steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Go to the **Themes** in **Admin Panel** and purchase your preferred `Frontend` or `Dashboard` themes.     
    <img src="/images/themes/t-1.png" />    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    Once you complete the purchase successfully, the Theme's `Buy Now` button will automatically change to `Install` button. 
    <img src="/images/themes/t-2.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    After successfully installing the Theme, you will now see `Activate` button on that same page.
    <img src="/images/themes/t-3.png" />
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step 4">
    That is it, you have now installed and activated your theme. You can also visit the Themes tab to switch between themes, including activating the default theme if needed.
    <img src="/images/themes/t-4.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5">
    <Check>
      You have successfully completed installing and activating your new theme.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
