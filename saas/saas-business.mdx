## Installation

Login as Admin, and go to the **Marketplace**, follow the steps below to **install** and **configure** SaaS Business extension. 

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Go to the **Plugins** tab in the **Admin Panel** and install **SaaS Business** extension.     
    <img src="/images/saas/saas/saas-1.png" />    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    After installation is completed, go to the **General Settings** page in Admin Panel and click on **Plugins** tab there. Click on **SaaS Business** tab there. 
    <img src="/images/saas/saas/saas-2.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    1. Turn on the checkboxes for **Enable/Disable SaaS feature**  
        <Note>If you don't turn on this checkbox, SaaS features listed above will not be available to anyone.</Note>
    2. After you are done with setup click on **Save** button.
    <img src="/images/saas/saas/saas-3.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4">
    Next, refresh your browser page, and you will see **Finance** section appear in the **Admin Panel** sidebar menu, from there you can control SaaS features such as configuration of Payment Gateways, creation of Subscription plans.
    <img src="/images/saas/saas/saas-4.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5">
    You now have completed the SaaS Business extension configuration. Refer to the addtional tabs under **SaaS Features** tab in the menu panel to configure rest of the SaaS features as needed.  
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
    Congratulations! You have successfully completed installation of SaaS Business extension for MagicAds.
</Check>
