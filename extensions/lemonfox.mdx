## Introduction

Davinci AI’s Lemonfox TTS integration unlocks advanced text to speech capabilities for your platform. With access to various languages and voices that Lemonfox supports, you can let your users use them at the AI Voiceover service. 

## Lemonfox Extension Setup
To be able to use your **Lemonfox** extension with **AI Voiceover** feature, follow these steps to setup your Lemonfox account.

<Steps>
<Step title="Step 1">
    Go to the **Marketplace** in **Admin Panel** and install **Lemonfox** extension.     
    <img src="/images/extensions/lemonfox/l-1.png" />    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    After installation is completed, go to **AI Settings** page in Admin Panel and click on **Extensions** tab there. Click on **Lemonfox** there. 
    <img src="/images/extensions/lemonfox/l-2.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Next, enter your **Lemonfox API Key**. Also make sure that **Lemonfox TTS Feature** checkbox is turned on. You can get your Lemonfox API key [here](https://lemonfox.ai).
    <img src="/images/extensions/lemonfox/l-3.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4">
    After you are done with all the settings, click on **Save** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5">
    Now you need to include Lemonfox voices into the list of allowed vendors for both, your free tier users and subscribers. For that, please refer to the detailed [AI Voiceover intructions](/core/voiceover)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 6">
  <Check>
Congratulations! You have successfully comleted the setup process of Lemonfox extension.
</Check>
 </Step>
</Steps>
