## Extension Configuration
To be able to use Nano Banana model in AI Images feature, follow these steps to setup Nano Banana Extension.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Go to the **Marketplace** in **Admin Panel** and install **Nano Banana** extension.     
    <img src="/images/extensions/nano-banana/nano-1.png" />    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    After installation is completed, go to **AI Settings** page in Admin Panel and click on **Extensions** tab there. Click on **Nano Banana** there. 
    <img src="/images/extensions/nano-banana/nano-2.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Go to your Gemini account, [sign up here](https://aistudio.google.com/)website, if you don't have an account yet, then sign up with them and get your **API Key** on your profile page after you successfully complete the registration process on their website using your valid credit card information.
    <Warning>Please note that your Gemini account, does not allow any image generation if you don't have any credit card linked as their free tier does not cover Nano banana model</Warning>
    <Note>Store your API key securely and never share it publicly. The key provides direct access to your account.</Note>
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step 4">
    Enter your **API Key** in the Extensions tab - Nano Bana page for **Google Gemini API key** field and click **Save** button. 
    <img src="/images/extensions/nano-banana/nano-3.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5">
    You now have completed the Nano Banana extension configuration. Proceed to the section below on how to use it. 
  </Step>
</Steps>

## AI Image Configuration
Davinci AI provides you full control on which image generation AI vendors you want your users to have access with in **AI Images** feature. 
Plus you can control it both for Free Tier users and for Subscribers based on their subcription plans. 

<Note>For Subscription plans you need to have an Extended License</Note>

1. Go to **AI Settings** page in Admin Panel and select **General AI Settings** page
2. Scroll to **AI Image Settings (For All Groups)** section and select **Google** among the list as shown on the image below and click **Save** button. 
<img src="/images/extensions/nano-banana/nano-4.png" /> 
3. Now all users can see Nano Banana model available in the **AI Images** features upon selecting target image models.
